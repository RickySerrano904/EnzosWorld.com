Uncle Wick has these weird tall sticks in his yard. He calls them “bamboo.” I
call them challenge accepted. I spotted one. Tall. Wobbly. Suspiciously stick-like.
I bit it. I tugged. I growled with every ounce of my 42-pound fury. The bamboo?
Didn’t budge. Not even a wiggle. I gave it everything. Dirt flew. My legs flailed.
Uncle Wick yelled something about “landscaping.” I walked away muddy, panting, and
proud. Bamboo: 1. Enzo: Also 1 (for effort).
