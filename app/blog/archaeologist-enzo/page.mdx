I was just sniffing around when the dirt spoke to me. “Dig here,” it whispered.
So I did. For science. For glory. For absolutely no reason at all. Uncle Wick
yelled, but I uncovered a stick, a rock, and something that looked like a soggy
hot dog bun. A successful expedition..
